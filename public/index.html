Fix RootFS related errors in your failed EC2 Linux instance

Sun, Jun 7, 2020 TLDR; If you face the RootFS related issue, try to check whether the AMI was having an older version of the Linux kernel. If it does, chroot into your volume and perform a kernel upgrade. Once done, detach the volume and create an AMI out of it. This will fix the 1/2 status checks issue. Context If you have worked with Amazon EC2 in the past, there might be a chance that sometimes the EC2 instance fails to launch after a fresh start/stop-start of the instance.
